    Animals on board
    Small animals must be carried in a taxi without any problems, as long as they can be transported securely in a bag or cage. Please inform us when ordering if you would like to carry animals with you. Luggage on board
    Normally, the same baggage can be carried in a taxi without surcharge without cost consequences as in an airplane.

    Our taxi chauffeurs are happy to help with loading and unloading. At most, a contribution to expenses can be charged for carrying it there and away.


    Taxi Ordinance

    Global Taxi Limmattal is subject to the tax clearance of Dietikon, which you can find here.
    Our prices are determined as mentioned above in the Taxi Ordinance of Dietikon.
    Here is a quote from the Taxiverodnung - Dietikon.
    Art.
    26 1) The City Council shall issue a tariff
    regulation in the sense of a maximum fare for fares, waiting time taxis and special services.

    The tax watch must be used to check the fare.
